Cool Facts and Stats about National Parks in the USA

Fun Facts

  • The first national park in the World is Yellowstone National Park, established in 1872.
  • The largest national park in the USA is Wrangell-St. Elias National Park in Alaska, which is larger than Switzerland.
  • Great Smoky Mountains National Park is the most visited national park in the United States.
  • Yosemite National Park is famous for its stunning granite cliffs, waterfalls, and giant sequoia trees. It's home to El Capitan, which is the Mecca for rock climbers.
  • Carved by the Colorado River, the Grand Canyon is one of the most impressive National Parks in the world. The canyon is over a mile deep and up to 18 miles wide.
  • Death Valley National Park in California is the hottest, driest, and lowest national park in the United States, known for its extreme desert environment.
